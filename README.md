I have been considering whether the mechanism of the Constitution AI, designed to create values, could be applied to improving performance through the safe and transparent self-improvement of AI.I'll experiment with a new mechanism I've devised for the Constitution AI system.

**Applying Claude's Constitutional AI framework, I devised a mechanism for AI self-improvement. While remaining under human control, AI can learn and grow by creating and accumulating “laws” based on a constitution, much like humans develop consistent memories through growth. Furthermore, this ‘law’ mechanism may enable easier, transparent explanations of “why the model gave this answer or thought this way.” Additionally, when wanting to improve part of an AI model, there may be no need for extensive retraining; simply modifying these “laws” could achieve significant improvements at a much lower cost.**
